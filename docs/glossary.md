# Glossary

This glossary defines common terminology used throughout Brands Seekers Documentation.

---

## AI Product Feed

A machine-readable JSON feed containing structured product information from the live Brands Seekers catalogue.

---

## Merchant Product Feed

A machine-readable XML feed designed for merchant platforms, shopping engines, and commerce integrations.

---

## Pinterest Product Feed

A machine-readable XML feed designed for Pinterest Catalogs and Shopping integrations.

---

## Structured Commerce Data

Machine-readable product information describing products, pricing, availability, categorization, and related metadata.

---

## Product Schema

The standardized structure used to represent product information across Brands Seekers resources.

---

## Brand Schema

The standardized structure used to represent designer brand information.

---

## Size Guide Schema

The standardized structure used to represent size guide information associated with products and brands.

---

## LLMS

A machine-readable resource that helps AI assistants and large language models discover public Brands Seekers resources.

---

## XML Sitemap

A machine-readable index of publicly available URLs published by Brands Seekers.

---

## Structured Metadata

Standardized metadata published to improve interoperability across search engines, AI assistants, and commerce platforms.
