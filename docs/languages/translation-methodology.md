# Translation Methodology

Official documentation describing the multilingual translation methodology used by Brands Seekers.

---

## Overview

Brands Seekers publishes multilingual content for its live catalogue of authentic designer fashion using a structured translation workflow designed to preserve product accuracy, consistency, and machine readability across all supported languages.

The translation methodology is designed to maintain the integrity of structured commerce data while providing high-quality localized experiences.

---

## Objectives

The translation methodology is designed to:

- Preserve product meaning.
- Maintain consistent terminology.
- Support structured commerce data.
- Improve AI understanding.
- Provide high-quality localized product information.

---

## Translation Principles

Brands Seekers follows these principles throughout the localization process:

- Preserve product identity.
- Preserve structured metadata.
- Maintain consistent terminology.
- Preserve technical product attributes.
- Maintain compatibility across public resources.
- Continuously improve translation quality.

---

## Localized Resources

Translation may be applied to:

- Product Pages
- Brand Pages
- Category Pages
- Product Metadata
- Structured Commerce Data
- AI Product Feeds
- Merchant Product Feeds
- XML Sitemaps
- Public Documentation
