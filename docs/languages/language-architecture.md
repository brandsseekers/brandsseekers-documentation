# Language Architecture

Official documentation describing the multilingual architecture of the Brands Seekers platform.

---

## Overview

Brands Seekers operates a multilingual commerce platform that publishes localized content while maintaining a single live catalogue of authentic designer fashion.

Every language shares the same underlying product catalogue, ensuring consistency across product information, structured commerce data, AI-ready resources, and machine-readable documentation.

---

## Architecture

The multilingual platform is designed around a centralized product catalogue with localized public resources generated for each supported language.

Each language may include:

- Product Pages
- Brand Pages
- Category Pages
- Size Guides
- AI Product Feed
- Merchant Product Feed
- XML Sitemaps
- robots.txt
- llms.txt
- Structured Metadata

---

## Resource Consistency

Brands Seekers maintains consistent identifiers and structured data wherever practical to improve interoperability across:

- Search Engines
- AI Assistants
- Commerce Platforms
- Integration Partners
- Research Systems

---

## Benefits

The multilingual architecture is designed to:

- Improve international discoverability.
- Increase structured data coverage.
- Support AI-native product understanding.
- Provide localized shopping experiences.
- Maintain a consistent global catalogue.
