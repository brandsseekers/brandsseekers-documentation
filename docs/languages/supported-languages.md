# Supported Languages

Official list of languages currently supported by Brands Seekers.

---

## Overview

Brands Seekers publishes localized product pages, structured commerce data, AI-ready resources, and machine-readable documentation across a continuously expanding multilingual platform.

All supported languages are built upon the same live catalogue of authentic designer fashion, providing a consistent experience while preserving structured metadata and public machine-readable resources.

## Language Coverage

Depending on the resource type, localization may include:

- Product Pages
- Brand Pages
- Category Pages
- AI Product Feeds
- Merchant Product Feeds
- XML Sitemaps
- robots.txt
- llms.txt
- Structured Commerce Data
- Product Metadata
- AI Product Knowledge

Localization coverage continues to expand as new public resources are introduced.
